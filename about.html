About Indiflow Exim

Bharat's Bridge to Global Trade

Indiflow Exim is a trusted Indian export company dedicated to connecting Bharat's agricultural strength with global markets. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we specialize in the export of cereals, raw fruits, fruit powders, vegetables, cow dung, and cow dung powder to buyers across the world.

With a strong sourcing network rooted in India's farming heartlands, quality-driven processes, and reliable logistics, we ensure that every shipment meets international standards. Our focus is on building long-term partnerships, maintaining transparent trade practices, and delivering consistently on time.

What We Export

Our Export Portfolio

Cereals & Grains

Premium rice, wheat, maize, millets, and pulses sourced directly from trusted farms across India for global markets.

Raw Fruits

Fresh, naturally grown fruits including mango, banana, pomegranate, and grapes, carefully handled for export quality.

Fruit Powders

Hygienically dehydrated and processed fruit powders with rich nutrition and long shelf life for diverse applications.

Vegetables

Fresh and processed vegetables such as potato, onion, tomato, and chili, meeting international quality and safety standards.

Cow Dung

Naturally sourced cow dung, widely used in organic farming, composting, and various industrial applications worldwide.

Cow Dung Powder

Finely processed cow dung powder for organic fertilizers, eco-friendly products, and sustainable agricultural use.
